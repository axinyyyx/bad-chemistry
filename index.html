How to Play

Drag the element cards from the bottom grid and drop them into the correct slots in the top grid.

Match the elements based on their symbol or name, depending on the level's mode.

Earn points for correct matches, but lose lives for incorrect ones. Complete the level before time runs out!

Hint: The correct elements will be revealed for 5 seconds.
