How to Play

Welcome to the Bad Chemistry! Learn all 118 elements by matching their symbols to their correct positions.

  • Objective: Click an element card (showing the symbol, e.g., "H") and then click the correct slot in the periodic table (showing the atomic number).
  • Lives: You have 3 lives. A wrong placement costs 1 life.
  • Score: Earn 1 point per correct placement. Get 3 correct placements in a row for a +2 combo bonus!
  • Hint: Click the Hint button to reveal a symbol for 5 seconds (once per level).
  • Timer: Complete the level within 2 minutes.
  • Levels:
    • Level 1: First 20 elements (Beginner).
    • Level 2: Metals and Non-Metals.
    • Level 3: Transition Metals.
    • Level 4: Rare Earths and Noble Gases.
    • Level 5: Master Infinity (full periodic table).
