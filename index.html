How to Play

Welcome to the Bad Chemistry! Learn all 118 elements by matching their symbols to their correct positions.

  • Objective: Click an element card (showing the symbol, e.g., "H") and then click the correct slot in the periodic table (showing the atomic number or "?").
  • Lives: You have 3 lives. A wrong placement costs 1 life.
  • Score: Earn 1 point per correct placement. Get 3 correct placements in a row for a +2 combo bonus!
  • Accuracy: Achieve 100% accuracy in each level to unlock the next. Accuracy is based on correct placements without errors.
