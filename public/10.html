Neon

Neon is the tenth element in the periodic table and is classified as a noble gas. It is colorless, odorless, and inert under most conditions.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 10
Symbol Ne
Atomic Mass 20.180 u
Electron Configuration 1s2 2s2 2p6
State at Room Temperature Gas

Interesting Facts About Neon

  • Neon emits a bright orange-red glow in neon signs.
  • It is the fifth most abundant element in the universe.
  • Neon is very rare on Earth, found in trace amounts in the atmosphere.
  • It is used in high-voltage indicators and vacuum tubes.
