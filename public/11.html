Sodium

Sodium is an alkali metal and is highly reactive. It is the sixth most abundant element in the Earth's crust.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 11
Symbol Na
Atomic Mass 22.990 u
Electron Configuration 1s2 2s2 2p6 3s1
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Sodium

  • Used in street lights to produce bright yellow light.
  • Sodium compounds, like table salt (NaCl), are essential in food and industry.
  • It is used as a heat transfer agent in nuclear reactors.
