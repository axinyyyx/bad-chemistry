Aluminum

Aluminum is a post-transition metal and the most abundant metal in the Earth's crust.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 13
Symbol Al
Atomic Mass 26.982 u
Electron Configuration 1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p1
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Aluminum

  • Used in packaging (e.g., aluminum foil) and lightweight vehicles.
  • Common in building materials like window frames.
  • Used in electrical transmission lines due to its conductivity.
