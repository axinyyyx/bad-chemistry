Sulphur

Sulphur is a non-metal known for its yellow appearance. It is essential for life and is found in amino acids and proteins.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 16
Symbol S
Atomic Mass 32.06 u
Electron Configuration 1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p4
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Sulphur

  • Used in the production of sulphuric acid, an important industrial chemical.
  • Plays a role in vulcanizing rubber.
  • Essential in fertilizers and pesticides.
