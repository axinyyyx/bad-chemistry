Chlorine

Chlorine is a halogen and a highly reactive gas. It is commonly used in disinfection and water treatment.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 17
Symbol Cl
Atomic Mass 35.45 u
Electron Configuration 1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p5
State at Room Temperature Gas

Uses of Chlorine

  • Used in disinfectants and water purification.
  • Vital in the production of PVC (polyvinyl chloride).
  • Used in bleach for cleaning and stain removal.
