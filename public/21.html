Scandium

Scandium is a transition metal, soft, and has a silvery-white appearance. It is used in aerospace and sports equipment.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 21
Symbol Sc
Atomic Mass 44.956 u
Electron Configuration 1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p6 3d1 4s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Scandium

  • Used in lightweight alloys for aerospace applications.
  • Improves the performance of sports equipment such as baseball bats and bicycles.
