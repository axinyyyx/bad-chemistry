Vanadium

Vanadium is a transition metal known for its strength and resistance to corrosion. It is used in alloys and has applications in the steel industry.

Symbol V

Uses of Vanadium

  • Used in the production of high-strength alloys, especially for steel.
  • Used as a catalyst in some chemical reactions.
