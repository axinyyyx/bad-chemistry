Copper

Copper is a reddish metal known for its excellent electrical and thermal conductivity. It is widely used in electrical wiring, plumbing, and coinage.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 29
Symbol Cu
Atomic Mass 63.546 u
Electron Configuration 1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p6 3d10 4s1
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Copper

  • Primarily used in electrical wiring and plumbing due to its excellent conductivity.
  • Commonly used in the production of coins and in various alloys.
