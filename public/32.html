Germanium

Germanium is a metalloid element used in semiconductors. It is an essential material in the production of transistors and other electronic components.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 32
Symbol Ge
Atomic Mass 72.63 u
Electron Configuration 1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p6 3d10 4s2 4p2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Germanium

  • Used in semiconductors, fiber-optic systems, and infrared optics.
  • Important in the production of transistors and solar cells.
