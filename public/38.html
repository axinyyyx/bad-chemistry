Strontium

Strontium is a soft, silvery-white alkaline earth metal used in fireworks, magnets, and in medical treatments such as for bone diseases.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 38
Symbol Sr
Atomic Mass 87.62 u
Electron Configuration 1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p6 4s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Strontium

  • Used in the production of fireworks for red coloring and in flares.
  • Used in medical treatments for bone diseases and in radiography.
  • Used in the manufacture of strong permanent magnets.
