Ruthenium

Ruthenium is a rare, precious metal often used in electrical contacts and in catalysis. It is a member of the platinum group of metals.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 44
Symbol Ru
Atomic Mass 101.07 u
Electron Configuration 1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p6 3d10 4s2 4d7
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Ruthenium

  • Used in electrical contacts and thick-film resistors.
  • Used in catalysis, particularly in petroleum refining and chemical processes.
  • Used in jewelry and as a coating for platinum and palladium.
