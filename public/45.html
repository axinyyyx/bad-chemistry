Rhodium

Rhodium is a rare, precious metal with a high reflectance. It is widely used in catalytic converters for cars, as well as in jewelry.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 45
Symbol Rh
Atomic Mass 102.91 u
Electron Configuration 1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p6 3d10 4s2 4d8
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Rhodium

  • Used in catalytic converters to reduce harmful emissions from vehicles.
  • Used in jewelry and high-end decorative items due to its lustrous finish.
  • Used in hydrogenation reactions in the chemical industry.
