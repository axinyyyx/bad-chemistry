Indium

Indium is a soft, malleable metal that is primarily used in electronics and soldering.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 49
Symbol In
Atomic Mass 114.82 u
Electron Configuration 1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p6 3d10 4s2 4d10 5s2 4p1
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Indium

  • Used in the production of touchscreens, flat-panel displays, and solar panels.
  • Used as an alloy to improve the strength and corrosion resistance of other metals.
  • Used in electronics for soldering and as a replacement for lead in various applications.
