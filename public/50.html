Tin

Tin is a soft, silvery-white metal that has been used for thousands of years in alloys, coatings, and packaging.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 50
Symbol Sn
Atomic Mass 118.71 u
Electron Configuration 1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p6 3d10 4s2 4d10 5s2 4p6 5d10 6s2 4f14 5p2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Tin

  • Used in the production of alloys, particularly bronze, and solder.
  • Used as a coating material for steel cans (tinplate) to prevent rusting.
  • Used in the manufacturing of glass and ceramics.
