Antimony

Antimony is a metalloid with applications in flame retardants, batteries, and the production of semiconductors.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 51
Symbol Sb
Atomic Mass 121.76 u
Electron Configuration 1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p6 3d10 4s2 4d10 5s2 4p6 5d10 6s2 5p3
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Antimony

  • Used in the production of flame retardants, particularly in plastics and textiles.
  • Used in lead alloys to improve strength and durability in batteries.
  • Used in semiconductors for various electronic devices.
