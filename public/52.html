Tellurium

Tellurium is a brittle, silvery-white metalloid used in semiconductors and alloys.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 52
Symbol Te
Atomic Mass 127.60 u
Electron Configuration 1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p6 3d10 4s2 4p6 4d10 5s2 5p4
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Tellurium

  • Used in alloys to improve durability and strength.
  • Used in thermoelectric devices to convert heat into electricity.
  • Used as a semiconductor material.
