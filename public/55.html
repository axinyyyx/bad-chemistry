Cesium

Cesium is a soft, golden alkali metal known for its high reactivity and use in atomic clocks.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 55
Symbol Cs
Atomic Mass 132.91 u
Electron Configuration 1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p6 3d10 4s2 4p6 4d10 5s2 5p6 6s1
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Cesium

  • Used in atomic clocks, which are the most accurate timekeeping devices.
  • Used in oil exploration as a drilling fluid additive.
  • Used in photoelectric cells and ion propulsion systems.
