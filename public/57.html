Lanthanum

Lanthanum is a rare earth metal and is often classified as one of the lanthanides.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 57
Symbol La
Atomic Mass 138.905 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 5d1 6s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Lanthanum

  • Used in the production of special glasses and ceramics.
  • Involved in the manufacture of hydrogen storage systems and catalytic converters.
