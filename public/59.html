Praseodymium

Praseodymium is a rare earth element that is mainly used in alloys and as a catalyst in the petroleum industry.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 59
Symbol Pr
Atomic Mass 140.907 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f3 6s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Praseodymium

  • Used in the production of high-strength alloys and magnets.
  • Applied in catalytic converters and as a glass colorant.
