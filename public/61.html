Promethium

Promethium is a rare earth element and is one of the few elements that is radioactive and does not have stable isotopes.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 61
Symbol Pm
Atomic Mass 145 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f5 6s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Promethium

  • Used in atomic batteries to power spacecraft and medical devices.
  • Can be used as a phosphor in certain types of lighting.
