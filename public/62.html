Samarium

Samarium is a rare earth metal that is used in high-performance magnets and in nuclear applications.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 62
Symbol Sm
Atomic Mass 150.36 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f6 6s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Samarium

  • Used in the production of permanent magnets for various applications, such as motors and generators.
  • Applied in nuclear reactors as a neutron absorber.
