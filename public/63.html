Europium

Europium is a rare earth element that is mainly used in phosphorescent and fluorescent applications, such as in TV and computer screens.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 63
Symbol Eu
Atomic Mass 151.98 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f7 6s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Europium

  • Used in phosphorescent and fluorescent applications, including in red phosphors for TV screens.
  • Applied in the production of certain types of lasers and high-performance alloys.
