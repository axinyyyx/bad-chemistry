Gadolinium

Gadolinium is a rare earth metal used in various applications including medical imaging and neutron radiography.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 64
Symbol Gd
Atomic Mass 157.25 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f7 5d1 6s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Gadolinium

  • Used in medical imaging, particularly in MRI contrast agents.
  • Applied in nuclear reactors as a neutron absorber.
