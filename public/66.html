Dysprosium

Dysprosium is a rare earth metal used in the production of high-performance magnets and in nuclear reactors.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 66
Symbol Dy
Atomic Mass 162.5 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f10 6s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Dysprosium

  • Used in the creation of high-performance magnets for electric vehicles and wind turbines.
  • Utilized in nuclear reactors as a neutron absorber.
