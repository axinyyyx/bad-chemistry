Holmium

Holmium is a rare earth metal that is used in nuclear reactors and in the creation of strong permanent magnets.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 67
Symbol Ho
Atomic Mass 164.93 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f11 6s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Holmium

  • Used in the production of high-performance magnets for medical devices and scientific instruments.
  • Utilized in nuclear reactors as a neutron absorber.
