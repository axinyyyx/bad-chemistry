Erbium

Erbium is a rare earth element that is commonly used in fiber optic technology and as a doping agent in solid-state lasers.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 68
Symbol Er
Atomic Mass 167.26 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f12 6s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Erbium

  • Used in fiber optics and lasers for communication and medical applications.
  • Used as a dopant in solid-state lasers and optical amplifiers.
