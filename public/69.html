Thulium

Thulium is a rare earth metal used in the creation of laser equipment, and in medical imaging.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 69
Symbol Tm
Atomic Mass 168.93 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f13 6s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Thulium

  • Used in lasers for medical and industrial applications.
  • Applied in the creation of certain medical imaging devices.
