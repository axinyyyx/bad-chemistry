Ytterbium

Ytterbium is a rare earth element used in certain lasers and in the production of high-performance alloys.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 70
Symbol Yb
Atomic Mass 173.04 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f14 6s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Ytterbium

  • Used in the creation of high-performance lasers for medical applications.
  • Applied in the production of certain alloys and in the process of welding.
