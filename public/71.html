Lutetium

Lutetium is a rare earth element with uses in positron emission tomography (PET) scanners and in high-temperature superconductors.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 71
Symbol Lu
Atomic Mass 175.00 u

State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Lutetium

  • Used in positron emission tomography (PET) scanners for medical diagnostics.
  • Employed in high-performance superconductors and in research applications.
