Hafnium

Hafnium is a ductile, silvery transition metal used in nuclear reactors and electronic devices.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 72
Symbol Hf
Atomic Mass 178.49 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f14 5d2 6s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Hafnium

  • Used in control rods for nuclear reactors.
  • Used in superalloys for jet engines.
  • Used in microprocessors for high-k dielectrics.
