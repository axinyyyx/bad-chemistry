Tantalum

Tantalum is a rare, corrosion-resistant metal used in electronics and medical implants.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 73
Symbol Ta
Atomic Mass 180.95 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f14 5d3 6s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Tantalum

  • Used in capacitors and resistors for electronics.
  • Used in alloys to enhance strength and corrosion resistance.
  • Used in medical implants, such as surgical instruments and bone repair devices.
