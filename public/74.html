Tungsten

Tungsten is a dense, hard metal known for its high melting point and use in electrical and military applications.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 74
Symbol W
Atomic Mass 183.84 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f14 5d4 6s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Tungsten

  • Used in filaments for incandescent light bulbs.
  • Used in cutting tools and military armor-piercing ammunition.
  • Used in alloys to enhance hardness and durability.
