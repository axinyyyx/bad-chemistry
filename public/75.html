Rhenium

Rhenium is a rare and dense metal used in superalloys, thermocouples, and catalysts.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 75
Symbol Re
Atomic Mass 186.21 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f14 5d5 6s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Rhenium

  • Used in superalloys for jet engines and gas turbines.
  • Used in thermocouples for high-temperature measurements.
  • Used as a catalyst in the production of lead-free, high-octane gasoline.
