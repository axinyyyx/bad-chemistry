Osmium

Osmium is a hard, dense metal known for its applications in alloys and fountain pen tips.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 76
Symbol Os
Atomic Mass 190.23 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f14 5d6 6s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Osmium

  • Used in alloys to create extremely hard and durable materials.
  • Used in electrical contacts and filaments.
  • Used in the tips of fountain pens and needles.
