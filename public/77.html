Iridium

Iridium is a dense and corrosion-resistant metal, primarily used in high-temperature and high-durability applications.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 77
Symbol Ir
Atomic Mass 192.22 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f14 5d7 6s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Iridium

  • Used in spark plugs and electrical contacts.
  • Used in crucibles for high-temperature processes.
  • Used in the standard kilogram weight definition.
