Platinum

Platinum is a precious, malleable metal widely used in jewelry, catalytic converters, and electronics.

Symbol Pt

Uses of Platinum

  • Used in catalytic converters for automobiles.
  • Used in jewelry and laboratory equipment.
  • Used in electrodes and fuel cells.
