Thallium

Thallium is a soft, malleable metal with toxic properties, often used in electronics and optics.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 81
Symbol Tl
Atomic Mass 204.38 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f14 5d10 6s2 6p1
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Thallium

  • Used in low-melting glass for optical purposes.
  • Used in electronics for semiconductor applications.
  • Used historically as a poison and in insecticides.
