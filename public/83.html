Bismuth

Bismuth is a brittle metal with low toxicity, often used in pharmaceuticals and alloys.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 83
Symbol Bi
Atomic Mass 208.98 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f14 5d10 6s2 6p3
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Bismuth

  • Used in pharmaceuticals like Pepto-Bismol.
  • Used in low-melting alloys and as a replacement for lead.
  • Used in cosmetics and pigments.
