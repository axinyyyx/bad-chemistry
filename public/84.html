Polonium

Polonium is a rare and highly radioactive element with limited applications.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 84
Symbol Po
Atomic Mass 209 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f14 5d10 6s2 6p4
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Polonium

  • Used in anti-static devices and brushes.
  • Used as a heat source in space missions.
  • Handled with extreme caution due to its high radioactivity.
