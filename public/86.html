Radon

Radon is a radioactive noble gas produced from the decay of uranium and thorium.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 86
Symbol Rn
Atomic Mass 222 u
Electron Configuration [Xe] 4f14 5d10 6s2 6p6
State at Room Temperature Gas

Uses of Radon

  • Used in cancer treatment for radiation therapy.
  • Studied for geological and environmental monitoring.
