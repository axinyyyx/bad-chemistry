Radium

Radium is a radioactive alkaline earth metal used in some medical and industrial applications.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 88
Symbol Ra
Atomic Mass 226 u
Electron Configuration [Rn] 7s2

Uses of Radium

  • Historically used in self-luminous paints.
  • Used in cancer treatment in earlier years.
