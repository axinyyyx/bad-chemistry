Thorium

Thorium is a radioactive element used as a fuel in nuclear reactors and in the production of certain types of glass.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 90
Symbol Th
Atomic Mass 232.04 u
Electron Configuration [Rn] 6d2 7s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Thorium

  • Used as a nuclear fuel in certain types of reactors.
  • Used in the production of high-temperature resistant materials, such as in lamps and glassmaking.
