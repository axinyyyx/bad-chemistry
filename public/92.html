Uranium

Uranium is a radioactive element primarily used as fuel in nuclear reactors and for producing nuclear weapons.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 92
Symbol U
Atomic Mass 238.03 u
Electron Configuration [Rn] 5f3 6d1 7s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Uranium

  • Used as fuel in nuclear reactors for energy production.
  • Used in the production of nuclear weapons and isotopic radiation sources.
