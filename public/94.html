Plutonium

Plutonium is a radioactive element used in nuclear reactors, nuclear weapons, and as a fuel in nuclear power plants.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 94
Symbol Pu
Atomic Mass 244 u
Electron Configuration [Rn] 5f6 6d1 7s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Plutonium

  • Used as fuel in nuclear reactors and as a power source in space exploration.
  • Used in nuclear weapons due to its highly radioactive properties.
