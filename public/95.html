Americium

Americium is a synthetic radioactive element used in smoke detectors and as a neutron source in scientific research.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 95
Symbol Am
Atomic Mass 243 u
Electron Configuration [Rn] 5f7 6d1 7s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Americium

  • Used in smoke detectors due to its ability to emit alpha radiation.
  • Employed in scientific research as a neutron source in various experiments.
