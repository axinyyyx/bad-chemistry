Californium

Californium is a synthetic radioactive element used in nuclear reactors and for neutron radiography.

Atomic Structure

Atomic Information

Property Value
Atomic Number 98
Symbol Cf
Atomic Mass 251 u
Electron Configuration [Rn] 5f10 6d1 7s2
State at Room Temperature Solid

Uses of Californium

  • Used in nuclear reactors to initiate fission reactions.
  • Used in neutron radiography for detecting flaws in materials.
